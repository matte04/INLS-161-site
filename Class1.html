EXSS 288

Emergency Care of Injuries and Illness is a class that I hope by the end I will be able to describe the common symptoms and care for common injuries and illnesses in athletics. The first part of the course is based on CPR certification, so I have been learning the proper techniques and training to officially become CPR certified. Over the next few months, I will learn about different types of injuries such as ACL or MCL tears and how to properly diagnose and care for them. While learning this information, I will also be going over basic human anatomy that will better allow me to do the things taught by this course. I hope to be a physical therapist one day, and I believe this course will be very helpful and influential on my future.
