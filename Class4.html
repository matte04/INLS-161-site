WMST 101

An introduction to women's and gender studies is a course that dives into the central political issues and questions raised and faced by feminists in the United States and in other countries. Some of the topics that get discussed in this class are how certain systems of inequality connect with each other and reinforce the same ideas, how patriarchy and masculinity has contributed to the oppression of women over time, and how feminists are working toward creating more of an equal society for all genders. By the end of this course, I hope to have a better understanding of the ideals behind feminism and help contribute to more equality in any way possible.
