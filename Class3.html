EXSS 175

Human Anatomy is a class that provides a systematic approach to the different systems of the body. The first part of the class focuses on the skeletal and articular systems of the body but towards the end it will move on to the cardiovascular and nervous systems. By the end of the course, I hope to obtain a solid foundation to build on anatomical knowledge combined with experience to be able to advance my studies to a graduate program. My goal is to become a physical therapist, so this class is very essential and resourceful to my ability to achieve that goal.
