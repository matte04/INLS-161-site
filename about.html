About Me

My name is Matthew Eller and I was born in 1997. I am from Greensboro, North Carolina, which is only an hour away from Chapel Hill. I attended Grimsley High School where my mother teaches Latin. During my lifetime I have always competitively played my two favorite sports, which are soccer and basketball. I initially started out as a biology major but this semester I decided to switch and become an Exercise and Sports Science major with the hope of going to graduate school for physical therapy. One interesting fact about me is that I've traveled to Europe five times because my mother takes students each year for spring break to Italy and a few other European countries to learn more about Latin and its origins.
